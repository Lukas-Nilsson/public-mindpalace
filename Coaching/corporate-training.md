## What We Cover

## Typical Program Structure

### Phase 1: Assessment (1-2 sessions)
- Current state audit: How is AI used now?
- Workflow analysis: Where are the opportunities?
- Team readiness: What's the learning curve?
- Goals: What outcomes are you targeting?

### Phase 2: Training (4-8 sessions)
- Group workshops: Core concepts and strategy
- One-on-one coaching: Individual workflow integration
- Hands-on practice: Real work applications
- Feedback loops: Measure and iterate

### Phase 3: Systems (Ongoing)
- Documentation: Build internal playbooks
- Peer learning: Create internal knowledge sharing
- Continuous improvement: Regular check-ins and updates
